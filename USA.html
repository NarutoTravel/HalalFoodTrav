The Halal food places of USA

Antalia NYC

When you head to Antalia NYC, you’ll be treated to the most chic shish kebabs. With its cool, laidback design, along with several non-vegetarian items, you will also be rewarded with the healthier options such as sandwiches stuffed with garden-fresh salads, and sweet-potato fries.

Address:17 W 45th St (between 5th Ave & 6th Ave), New York, NY 10036, United States

Opening Hours:11 AM– 10 PM

Phone:+1 212-221-3333

What to order: Kebabs

Junoon

If you are carving for a fancy meal in the Big Apple, then look no further than Junoon, a Michelin-starred Indian restaurant that can cater for those who follow a halal diet. The restaurant also offers traditional dishes with a twist such as an eggplant chaat, ghost chili chicken tikka, and lamb boti kebab.

Address:27 W 24th St, New York, NY 10010, USA

Opening Hours:12– 3 PM, 5:30– 10:30 PM

Phone:+1 212-490-2100

What to order:Chicken Tikka and lamb boti kebab
